Common Questions

What is Revonn? expand_more
Revonn is the AI Business Operating System for India — built to help businesses run billing, inventory, customer management, dues, finance, and insights from one connected platform. It is designed not just to record business activity, but to help owners act faster, work smarter, and grow with confidence.
Is Revonn secure for my financial data? expand_more
Revonn uses enterprise-grade 256-bit encryption and multi-region backups. We are ISO 27001 and GDPR compliant, ensuring your data remains private and protected.
Do I need an internet connection to use Voice? expand_more
Most core voice processing requires a connection for real-time AI analysis. However, basic billing and offline data entry are supported and will sync once back online.
Can I migrate my data from Tally or Excel? expand_more
Absolutely. We have built-in importers for Tally, Busy, and custom Excel sheets. Our team also provides assisted migration for premium enterprise clients.
Does Revonn support voice commands and multiple languages? expand_more
Yes. Revonn is built to understand natural voice and text commands in Hindi, Hinglish, English and 22+ languages so business owners can operate the system the way they naturally speak. From creating bills to checking sales, updating stock, and tracking dues, Revonn turns everyday language into business action.
How does Revonn help businesses grow? expand_more
Revonn goes beyond management by helping owners spot opportunities and problems early. It can highlight pending dues, identify low-stock and slow-moving products, surface customer follow-up opportunities, and help businesses make faster, smarter decisions that support revenue growth.
Can Revonn handle billing, stock, customer records, and dues together? expand_more
Yes. Revonn connects the key parts of the business into one flow, so updates across billing, stock, customer records, and dues stay aligned. This reduces manual work and gives the owner a clearer picture of the business at all times.
Is Revonn made for Indian businesses? expand_more
Absolutely. Revonn is built around how Indian businesses actually work — with multilingual usage, practical workflows, WhatsApp-style communication, due tracking, and natural business language. It is designed to feel simple, useful, and familiar even for non-technical users.
